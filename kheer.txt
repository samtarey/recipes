Ingredients:
     - thin Indian noodles
     - clarified butter
     - Milk
     - Condensed milk
     - Sugar
     - Cardamom powder
     - Cashews, almonds, pistachios(all chopped)


Directions:

1. Fry noodles in ghee till golden brown.
2. Add milk. Add nuts.  Stir occassionally after milk starts boiling. Reduce 1/3 or till the noodles cooks perfectly.
3. Add condensed milk. simmer for 5 min after the kheer reaches boiling point again.
4. Add sugar. Simmer for 2 more minutes.
5. Add cardamom powder.
